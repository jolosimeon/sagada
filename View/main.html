    When I first visited Sarasota County - as a 23 year old Iowan with very little travel outside the Midwest - it didn't take me more than a few hours to find three reasons to fall in love with Sarasota County. I bet it won't take you much longer.

    Authentic. Traveling to the downtown area of the city of Sarasota, my first discovery was a colorful scene of niche shops, eateries, bars and music venues lining the streets from Burns Court through Palm Avenue and Main Street. I knew this was a place I could enjoy a meal at one of the many sidewalk patios and spend the afternoon people-watching.

    Stunning. While downtown was beautiful in its own right, traveling over the Ringling Causeway provided a stunning image of the glittering bay. It was a powerful confirmation that Sarasota County has some of the most gorgeous views in the world.

    Serene. My first beach excursion, to Caspersen Beach, was in the middle of August, however, the sand was still cool to the touch despite the sweltering heat. As I strolled down the beach searching for shark's teeth and enjoying the sound of the azure blue waves rolling back and forth, I knew I had come to the right place.

    We hope you find your own reasons to love Sarasota County. Use this guide to help you explore our destination from your mobile device.

    Eddie Kirsch, Visit Sarasota County
